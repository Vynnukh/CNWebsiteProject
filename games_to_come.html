Games to Come


Galactic Chronicles


Wars Of The Galaxy I

The first Galactic wide conflict on the franchise, involving the conflicts between several worlds and unvaeling a hidden corruption of which seeks to bring doom to all. You can choose which factions to support throughout this game, and discover the truth of which lurks beneath the shadows


Wars Of The Galaxy II

A continuation of Wars Of The Galaxy I, detailing how the galaxy continues after the corruptions have been quelled (Or have they?), Find out in this continuation of this galactic conflict. In this story, you will take on the role of a rebel who is against the Rrallkon Empire, set to bring about the downfall of Empress Chrella. But as you begin your journey, you soon find that even other powers within the empire are against their zealous Empress.

Chronicles Of Terria

The Chronicles of Terria detail the events surrounding the planet of Terria, a world of which a group of refugees from very different backgrounds sought to begin their own colonies. At first these groups were able to work together, but eventually, rising tensions begin to cause problems.

Conflict Of Altaeus


The Continent of Altaeus, a place where many species from around the galaxy came to create a peaceful civilisation. But thing have gone arry, and a conflict has broken out across Altaeus, after the governing authority has been found to be corrupted, and caused chaos to break out across the continent. You will take on the role of a member from one of the factions of which is against the Authority of Altaeus, and together with the other factions is seeking to put an end to their madness.


Chronicles Of Isle Khretul

Conflict Against Tyrrany

A Rebellion has arisen against the Tyrant Overlord of Khretul, you uwill take on the role as a rebel fighting to bring down this tyrant, gathering allies and resourses across the Isle Of Khretul the rebellion requires to defeat him and put an end to his Tyrrany.


Unification Of Khretul

A look at the Isle of Khretul after the rebellion defeated the Tyrant Overlord Urzhael, exploring the Kingdom they created, and the problems they encountered throughout their rule. In This story, you will take on the role of a member of this civilisation. This game will be designed to ensure that the player can adopt a great many roles throughout this kingdom.


Fall Of The Khretul Isle Kingdom

This game will explore the eventual fate of the Kingdom of Khretul. You will either take on the role of a soldier loyal to the Khretul Kingdom, or a rebel who supports Threlsus' coup attempt.

Chronicles Of Verserious

The Conflicts Of Verserious

A prologue exploring the conflicts of which many colonies across Verserious had before the Rrallkon brought peace to the planet. This will detail how the newly formed Kallieshk Empire, the Gijuulk Alliance, the Richlythzkian Republic and the Azkylan Empire reacted to each other, after each chosing to form their own independant colonies upon Verserious. You will take on the role of a member of any of these factions, and begin a campaign of which will give you an insight into these factions.


The Dawn Of The Rrallkon Empire

Exploring the way the Rrallkon empire reacted towards the Verserian conflicts, and how they brought peace to Verserious. In this story, you will become a member of the Rrallkon Empire, and help them bring peace to Verserious.


The Aftermath After The Rrallkon Empire

This will show how Verserious was after the fall of the Rrallkon Empire, and how the survivors of such an extensive conflict of which almost consumed Verserious itself, manged to perservere. You will take on the role of a survivor from Verserious in this story, and embark upon a journey that will provide insight into how the denizens of Verserious continued to prosper, even after their planet was largely decimated by the Galactic Conflicts.
