Galactic Chronicles


Galactic Chronicles is a set of stories of which take place across an entire galaxy, ranging from extensive galactic wide conflicts to small rebellions on remote islands of individual worlds.. This franchise will see multiple games released, starting with the small mini game "Escape The Island".


So far, Galactic Chronicles will expore many stories surrounding the planet's of Terria and Verserious. In addition, it will explore an extensive galactic conflict of which includes many others worlds including both Verserious and Terria. These Conflicts will also dip into other worlds such as Zehre, Rilk-Xiev, Palznik, and many more...


Verserious

Explore Verserious, the seat of the Rrallkon Empire. where you will see the inner workings of their empire, as well as being able to explore the floating Isle of Versailos.From the unification of Verserious under the rule of the Rrallkon empire to the aftermath following their downfall, the Verserian Chronicles will take a look at the events surrounding this planet, and it's eventual fate.

The Planet of Verserious is quite old, and has many scattered ruins across it of which date back to a time before any of the many Galactic societies that currently exist coloniesed here.

Isle Of Versailos


Terria

Investingate the planet of Terria, where many species sought refuge from the galactic wide conflict. From the corrupted authority of Altaeus to the downfall of Empress Hyrrellza and her remnants of the Rrallkon Empire.

The planet of Terria is most notable for containing the most extensive archives in the galaxy with in it's interior.


Zehre

Zehre, a colony world origionally set up by an expedition of Dijolkian's. Once the Galactic conflicts start however, their colony is quickly transformed into a military fortification. Leading to their planet becoming a primary target for enemies of the Rrallkon Empire.
Zehre's red clouds are formed by an unusual phenomenon which occurs within the planet's atmosphere.


Rilk-Xiev

Rilk-Xiev, The third homeworld of the Kallieshk, after their first, Palznik was lost during "Wars Of The Galaxy I", and their second, Sylanius was destroyed by their enemies during "Wars Of The Galaxy II". Rilk-Xiev became the seat of the Kallieshk Empire following the destruction of their second world.

Rilk-Xiev's purple aura comes from powerful energies which constantly radiate from the planet's interior.


Palznik

Palznik, the origional world of the Kallieshk, of which was lost when a prototype created from the specifications of an ancient artefact goes wrong in "Wars Of The Galaxy I".

The Planet of Palznik was a highly influential trading hub during the height of the Kallieshk Empire's reign here.
